Why work with me

I've sat in the chair you're in.

For more than 20 years I've led people — New York, New Jersey, Virginia, Washington DC, Miami, and now the Treasure Coast. Teams as small as five and as large as five hundred. Ten of those years I spent owning my own entertainment company, so I know exactly what it feels like when the whole thing runs through you and there's no one to escalate to. I've made just about every mistake a leader can make — and then went and got a degree in Organizational Leadership, which mostly taught me the names for the mistakes I'd already made.

That's the point of the work. I'm not here to keep you from making mistakes — I'm here to bring them to the surface so you navigate them better than I ever did. I've personally developed more than 200 people to lead differently, and since launching Lead to Leave I've watched clients break through things no one else could help them move — not only because of the unique framework, but because of completely uninterrupted 1:1 time. That does wonders for anyone.

One promise up front: I'm not here to feed your ego, back a bad idea, or cheer on what you're already doing. If you're coming to me, it's because something isn't working — and you want to fix it the right way the first time. That's the only reason to do this.
