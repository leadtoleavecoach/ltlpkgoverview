Straight answers

The questions owners actually ask

How is this different from every leadership course I've already bought?

Most give you theory and leave you to figure out the "how." Every framework I teach was rebuilt until it had real action steps. Each Foundation ends with one rep — a specific thing to do with a real person on your team before you move on. Insight isn't change. Reps are.

I'm already stretched thin. How much time is this going to cost me?

Sessions are one focused hour. The self-paced Foundations are 15–20 minutes each, watched on your schedule. The entire point is to make you less of a bottleneck, so you get time back — not lose it.

I don't have a big team. Is this still for me?

Yes. Whether you lead 2 people or 20, the skill is the same: lead yourself first, then lead others on purpose instead of by reaction. Smaller teams usually feel the cost of winging it faster, because there's nowhere for the work to hide.

What if I put my managers through this and they leave?

Fair question — and the honest answer is that untrained managers leave more often than trained ones. People don't quit because you invested in them. They quit because nobody did. That's the whole idea behind LEAVE: they could go anywhere, and they stay because of how you lead.

Can I start small and move up later?

That's how most owners do it. Start with a Strategy Session or LIFT, see whether the way I work fits you, and step up when it's earned. If you upgrade to the Coaching Container or a Team Cohort within 60 days, I'll credit what you already spent toward it.

What if I do the work and nothing changes?

Then we look at it honestly and I tell you why — including if the reason is me. I'd rather lose the engagement than keep taking money for something that isn't working. That's also why every engagement ends with a real scorecard instead of a victory lap.

Virtual or in person?

All 1:1 coaching and cohort sessions are virtual, so distance isn't a factor. On-site intensives are available inside Organization Build, with travel quoted separately.

What happens after I book?

You'll get a confirmation and a short intake, so I show up already understanding your situation. Your time is too valuable for me to spend the first session getting up to speed.
